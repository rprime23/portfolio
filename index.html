About Me

My name is Reyna Murphy, and I am a student at the University of Utah. I am majoring in Computer Scince with an emphasis in games. I enjoy the problem solving aspect of programming, finding a problem and working to create a solution. I am organized, a team player, a leader, and someone who believes that asking questions is one of the most important thing you can do to better yourself.

More About Me

I believe that direct interaction can make the biggest difference in someone's life or day. As such, I aspire to make a difference in the people around me while doing what I enjoy. I find joy in watching a project come together and watching as the hard work you put in becomes something that can touch more people than you thought possible.

I became interested in computers after my brother built his own PC. Since then, I have discovered an interest in 3D modeling, programming, software development, and game development. To me, learning and understanding is the most important, and I am always striving to learn more and be introduced to more.

My hobbies include gaming, martial arts, and playing with my dogs.

My top skills are C++, C#, Java, Unreal Engine, and VS Code.
